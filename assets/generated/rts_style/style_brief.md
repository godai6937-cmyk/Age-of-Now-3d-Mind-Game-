# RTS Style Brief

- Buildings lean into Age-of-Empires-style readability: broad silhouettes, compact footprints, and strong faction color accents.
- Materials follow the references: warm sandstone, sun-bleached plaster, glazed blue domes, red market cloth, aged timber, and darker fortress stone.
- Character concepts use readable three-quarter silhouettes with cloth, armor, and cavalry variants to guide later sprite work.
- This local pack is a handcrafted fallback because browser AI generation was rate-limited during execution.
